Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Nexera AR platform.

How do I get started with Nexera AR?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free account, explore our template library, and start creating your first AR experience. No coding or technical skills required. We also offer video tutorials to guide you through the process.

What devices are compatible with Nexera AR?

Nexera AR works on most modern smartphones and tablets running iOS 12+ or Android 8.0+. For the best experience, we recommend devices released within the last 3 years. Desktop browsers are supported for creating content.

Can I share my AR projects with students?

Absolutely! You can share your AR experiences via QR codes, direct links, or embed them in your learning management system. Students can view and interact with your content without needing their own Nexera account.

What's included in the Student plan?

The Student plan includes access to premium templates, up to 20 projects, HD exports, and collaboration tools. You'll need to verify your student status with a valid student ID or institutional email address.

Do you offer training and support?

Yes! We provide comprehensive video tutorials, documentation, and email support. Standard plan users get priority support, and we also offer live training webinars monthly for all users.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period, and you can export all your projects before your subscription ends.

How do I upload custom 3D models?

Standard and Student plan users can upload custom 3D models in common formats like OBJ, FBX, and GLTF. Simply drag and drop your files into the editor, and our platform will optimize them for AR viewing.
